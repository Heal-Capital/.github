### About

Heal Capital is a Berlin-based VC fund founded in 2019, focused exclusively on **European healthtech**. We are backed by 25+ leading German private health insurers — including Allianz, AXA, DKV, Signal Iduna, Debeka, and HUK-Coburg.

This unique LP base gives our portfolio companies a direct line to the payers and distributors shaping European healthcare — turning capital into commercial traction.

Our exits include **Siilo → Doctolib** and **Formel Skin → Manual**.

---

### What We Build

Beyond investing, we build internal tools that make us sharper investors.

- **[HealthSignal](https://health-signal.com)** — A regulatory intelligence engine tracking 859+ regulations across 63 EU sources, built to keep founders and investors ahead of the regulatory curve.
- **AI Tooling** — We experiment with AI agents to improve how we source, evaluate, and support companies.
