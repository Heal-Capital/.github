### Who we are

Heal Capital is a Berlin-based venture capital fund investing in **European healthtech** from Pre-Seed to Series A. Founded in 2019, we manage **€200M+** across two funds, backed by **25+ leading German private health insurers** — including Allianz, AXA, DKV, Signal Iduna, Debeka, and HUK-Coburg.

We invest **€1–5M** in founders solving real problems in healthcare — not hype cycles. Our LP base gives portfolio companies a unique advantage: direct access to the payers and distributors of healthcare innovation.

### What we look for

```
 Platform over point solution.
 Traction over pitch decks.
 10x change over incremental improvement.
 Boring and non-conform over trendy.
 Scale beyond one market.
```
